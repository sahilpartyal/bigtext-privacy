1 Introduction

BigText ("we", "our", or "us") is developed by Sahil Partyal. This Privacy Policy explains how BigText handles information when you use our mobile application on iOS and Android. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

By using BigText, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you disagree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of the application.

2 Information We Collect & How We Use It

🕐 Google AdMob Ads

Free users see banner advertisements powered by Google AdMob. AdMob may automatically collect:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA / GAID)
  • IP address
  • App usage and interaction data
  • Device hardware & OS information

This data is used solely for ad personalisation and fraud prevention by Google. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

🛍 In-App Purchases

BigText offers optional subscription plans (monthly, yearly, lifetime) processed entirely through:

  • Apple App Store (iOS)
  • Google Play (Android)

We never receive, process, or store your payment details. All billing is handled exclusively by Apple or Google under their respective terms.

💾 Local Storage

Your display preferences are saved locally on your device using SharedPreferences:

  • Font family & size
  • Text & background colours
  • Display mode settings

This data never leaves your device. There is no cloud sync, no account, and no server-side storage.

🖼 Image Picker (Pro)

Pro users may upload a custom logo image to personalise the app.

  • Selected images are stored locally only
  • Images are never uploaded to any server
  • Requires photo library access permission which you can revoke at any time in device Settings

💡 Wakelock

BigText uses a wakelock to keep your screen on while displaying text — for example during a presentation. This is a system-level feature only. No data is collected through this functionality.

3 What We Don't Collect

✅ We never collect any of the following

  • Name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information
  • Location data (GPS, coarse location, or IP-based location)
  • Camera or microphone data
  • Contacts, calendar, or other sensitive device data
  • Analytics, usage statistics, or crash reports
  • Browsing history or cross-app activity
  • Any data transmitted to our servers (we don't operate any)

5 Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties, because we do not collect personal data in the first place. Any data collected by third-party SDKs (e.g. AdMob) is governed solely by those third parties' own policies.

We may disclose information if required by law or valid legal process, though given the minimal data we hold, such disclosure would be extremely limited in scope.

6 Data Retention & Deletion

All data stored by BigText (preferences, logo image) resides entirely on your device. You can delete this data at any time by:

  • Clearing app data — via your device's Settings > Apps > BigText > Clear Data
  • Uninstalling the app — removes all locally stored data
  • Resetting preferences — using the reset option within the app (if available)

For data collected by third-party services (AdMob), please refer to Google's data deletion options in their privacy policy.

7 Children's Privacy

BigText is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete such information.

8 Regulatory Compliance

GDPR (EU/EEA)
CCPA (California)
Google Play Policies
Apple App Store Guidelines

GDPR (EU Users): Because we do not collect personal data directly, most GDPR obligations do not apply to us. For data processed by AdMob, Google acts as an independent data controller. You may opt out of personalised advertising through your device's advertising settings.

CCPA (California Users): We do not sell personal information. California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected — in our case, it is none on our end. For AdMob data, please refer to Google's CCPA disclosures.

Opt-out of personalised ads:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking > disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > "Delete advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalisation"

9 Security

Since BigText stores all user data locally on your device and does not transmit it to external servers, your data security is primarily governed by your device's own security features (device encryption, screen lock, etc.). We encourage you to keep your device and operating system up to date.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this page. For significant changes, we may notify you via an in-app notice. Continued use of BigText after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we handle information.
